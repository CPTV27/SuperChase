## TIER 1: HIGH-VALUE SYNERGIES

### Scan2Plan ↔ Tuthill Design
**Connection Type:** Service Integration
**Opportunity Score:** 9/10

**How it works:**
- Tuthill clients renovating historic or complex homes need accurate as-builts
- Scan2Plan provides engineering-grade documentation Tuthill can design from
- Tuthill referrals become Scan2Plan projects; Scan2Plan projects become design leads

**Revenue potential:** 
- Average Scan2Plan project: $8,500
- Average Tuthill project: $45,000
- Combined package: $50,000+ with stickier client relationship

**Cross-sell script:**
*"Before we begin any renovation design, we capture the space with engineering-grade precision. Our sister company Scan2Plan creates documentation that prevents the costly surprises that derail timelines and budgets."*

**Implementation:**
1. Add Scan2Plan mention to Tuthill discovery process
2. Create joint case study showing scan-to-design workflow
3. Bundle pricing for renovation clients
