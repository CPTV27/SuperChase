# The Feed - Issue Template

## Subject Line Formula
"The Feed #[XXX]: [Hook that creates curiosity]"

Examples:
- "The Feed #001: The sound that's about to be everywhere"
- "The Feed #002: Why your algorithm is lying to you"
- "The Feed #003: I found the next Doja Cat"
