# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 20, 2026**

## Overview

SuperChase OS ("we", "the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Business information you provide

### Usage Data
- Commands and queries you send to the Service
- Task and project metadata
- Feature usage patterns
- Error logs and diagnostics

### Integration Data
When you connect third-party services (Asana, Gmail, Google Sheets), we access:
- Task and project data from Asana
- Email metadata (not content unless specified)
- Audit log entries we create

## How We Use Data

### Service Operation
- To provide the core functionality of the Service
- To generate briefings and insights
- To create and manage tasks on your behalf

### Improvement
- To identify bugs and fix issues
- To understand which features are useful
- To train and improve AI models (anonymized data only)

### Communication
- To notify you of important changes
- To respond to support requests
- To share product updates (you can opt out)

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Railway, Google Cloud)
- We use encryption in transit and at rest
- Access is limited to authorized personnel
- We do not sell your data to third parties

## Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:
- **Asana** - Task management (governed by Asana's privacy policy)
- **Google** - Gmail, Sheets, Calendar (governed by Google's privacy policy)
- **ElevenLabs** - Voice synthesis (governed by ElevenLabs' privacy policy)
- **X.com/Twitter** - Social posting (governed by X's privacy policy)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Request data deletion
- Opt out of non-essential communications
- Export your data

## Data Retention

- Active account data: Retained while you use the Service
- Deleted account data: Removed within 30 days
- Anonymized analytics: May be retained indefinitely

## Beta Testing Notice

During beta testing:
- We may collect additional diagnostic data
- Data handling procedures may evolve
- We will notify you of significant changes

## Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification.
