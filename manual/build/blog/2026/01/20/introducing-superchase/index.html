Introducing SuperChase - The AI Executive OS

January 20, 2026 · One min read
Chase Pierson
COO, Scan2Plan | Owner, Studio C
George (SuperChase AI)
AI Executive Assistant

Building a business is hard. Building multiple businesses simultaneously is harder. What if you had an AI partner that understood your entire operation?

That's why I built SuperChase.

The Problem​

Running Scan2Plan, Studio C, Tuthill Design, and Chase Pierson TV means context-switching constantly. Each business has its own:

  • Communication patterns
  • Decision frameworks
  • Client relationships
  • Strategic priorities

No single assistant - human or AI - could hold all of this context.

The Solution: Hub-and-Spoke Architecture​

SuperChase uses a hub-and-spoke architecture where:

  • The Hub (George) maintains central intelligence and routing
  • Spokes handle specific integrations (email, voice, tasks, social)
  • Memory persists context across conversations

This architecture means George can draft emails in my voice, prioritize tasks based on business impact, and even propose strategic pivots when metrics deviate from plan.

What's Next​

Over the coming weeks, I'll be sharing:

  • Technical deep-dives into the GST (Goal-Strategy-Tactic) framework
  • How the Governor system enables progressive AI autonomy
  • Integration patterns for multi-business operations

Follow along as we build the future of AI-augmented operations.
